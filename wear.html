A lot of love goes into what I make...


I mostly make bracelets as I think they the most versatile, and apart from rings, they are the piece of jewellery that you always see. I like seeing my mine as I type away at my computer or write in my book. I choose the crystals in my jewellery very carefully depending on their healing properties, and also make sure they look attractive. I choose to make my bracelets with strong jewellery elastic as I find clasps extremely fiddly to use. Due to poor grip I can not always fasten jewellery by myself. I do not always have someone on hand who can help me fasten jewellery, and I think my bracelets suit those in the same situation. It is very important for me that my bracelets are easy to put on and taken off. All my bracelets have a lifetime guarantee and should they break, they can returned to me and I will be happy to repair them.


I like to use sterling silver to make my jewellery as silver enhances the healing properties of the crystals. I know there a people out there who have allergies to silver, therefore the * marked bracelets can be remade with stainless steel beads. I carefully source my stones to ensure they are genuine, AAA quality, ethically mined and shaped gemstones. I cleanse and enrich each piece with Reiki. I offer a gift wrapping service and can add a message to any order at no extra cost. The bracelets come wrapped in tissue paper and placed carefully inside a specially folded Origami pouch.


All of my jewelry can be bought through my shop on Folksy but feel free to browse some of my pieces below and get in touch if you would like to place a custom or wholesale order.
